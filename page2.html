How to Choose the Right Insurance Plan

Secure your loved ones’ future with the perfect coverage.

Choosing the right term insurance plan is key to securing your loved ones' future. There are different types of life and health insurance policies available, and term insurance is one of the most affordable options.

Key Things to Consider

1. Coverage Amount

One of the most important aspects is determining the required coverage amount. Often, people get a set amount that can cover hospitalization, surgery, emergency care, and more. We recommend multiplying your annual income by 10 to 15 times.

2. Hospitalization & Surgery

Make sure the plan covers confinement, surgeries, and major medical treatments.

3. Outpatient Benefits

Look for coverage on check-ups, lab tests, Annual Physical Examination, and Dental Care.

4. Emergency Care & Life Insurance

Good plans should include emergency medical care and life/accident protection.

5. Policy Term

Choose the right duration that matches your needs and goals.

Recommended Policy Terms

1-Year Plan

A good choice for individuals who want short-term medical coverage and flexibility. You can renew or adjust your plan yearly based on your needs.

10-Year Plan

Ideal for individuals or families who want long-term healthcare protection and stable coverage.

20-Year Plan

A great option for families who want lasting healthcare security and peace of mind.
